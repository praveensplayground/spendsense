How it works

Three steps.
Zero effort.

SpendSense turns your bank's transaction alerts into a clear financial picture — completely automatically.

1

Bank sends a transaction SMS

You make a payment. Your bank sends their usual alert — "Your account has been debited Rs. 850 at Keells Super."

2

SpendSense reads and categorises it

The amount, merchant, and category are extracted on your device the instant the SMS arrives. No server involved. No delay.

3

You see your spending clearly

Open the app to see where your money went — by day, by category, and against the monthly budgets you've set.

Privacy by design

Your data never leaves
your phone.

SpendSense was built with a single constraint: no servers. Your financial life is private by architecture, not just by policy.

0 Bytes uploaded Your transaction data never touches a server. Not even once.
0 Third-party SDKs No analytics, no crash reporters, no advertising frameworks.
0 Accounts needed No email, no login, no registration of any kind required.
0 Ads. Ever. SpendSense will never show you advertisements.

Technical note: SpendSense sets android:allowBackup="false" — your data cannot be silently uploaded to Google's cloud backup. In production builds, raw SMS content is never stored after parsing. Only the extracted fields — amount, merchant, category, date — are saved to your local database. Read the full privacy policy →
